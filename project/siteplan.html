Discover Delta State, Nigeria

1. Site Name

Site Name: Discover Delta State

The name “Discover Delta State” was chosen to invite visitors to explore the beauty, culture, food, and attractions of Delta State, Nigeria. It reflects the goal of the website — to showcase Delta’s heritage and tourism potential to a global audience.

2. Site Purpose

The website aims to promote Delta State as a top travel destination in Nigeria. It will provide information about the state’s rich culture, local cuisines, traditional festivals, and popular attractions. Visitors will learn about where to go, what to do, and how to experience Delta’s unique blend of tradition and modern life.

3. Scenarios

  • “I’m planning a visit to Delta State — what are the top tourist attractions I shouldn’t miss?”
  • “What traditional dishes and festivals is Delta State known for?”

4. Color Schema

The selected colors are inspired by the Delta State flag and nature.

Primary Color — Blue (#005F99)

Used for headings, buttons, and highlights.

Accent Color — Green (#007F5C)

Used for links, accents, and decorative elements.

Background — Light Cream (#F9F9F9)

Used for page background to maintain readability.

5. Typography

Font Family: Roboto

  • Headings: Roboto Bold
  • Body Text: Roboto Regular
  • Buttons/Highlights: Roboto Medium

This font was chosen for its readability, simplicity, and modern design, which aligns with the theme of an informative and accessible tourism website.
